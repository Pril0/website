Let's Crochet!



  • 3.5mm Crochet Hook
  • 2 Yarn Colors
  • Polyfil/ Filler
  • Glue Gun
  • Crochet Needle
  • Stitch Marker


  • mr: Magic Ring
  • slst: Slip Stitch
  • ch: Chain
  • st(s): Stitch(es)
  • sc: Single Crochet
  • hdc: Half Double Crochet
  • dc: Double Crochet

We will be working on a spiral, so weâ€™ll need a stitch marker to identify the first stitch of each round.


Round1: Magic Ring 6sc around the magic ring(6sts)
Round2: 2sc on each st around(12sts)
Round3: 1sc on one st and 2sc on next st around(18sts)
Round4: 1sc on two sts, 2sc on the next st around(24sts)
Round5: 1sc on three sts, 2sc on the next st around(30sts)
Round6-18: 1sc on each st around(30sts)
After Round18. Fasten off leaving a long tail. Add a tapestry needle to the long tail of yarn left.
Stuff with polyfil. Fold the opening as shown and sew the top.
