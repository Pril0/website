Crocheting is an ancient craft dating back to the early 19th century, originated from the French word "crochet," meaning hook. Initially used to create delicate lace trimmings, crocheting evolved into a popular pastime and practical skill, with intricate patterns and designs passed down through generations, showcasing the artistry and creativity of crafters worldwide

Over time, crocheting has transcended generations and cultures, becoming a beloved form of self-expression and a timeless craft cherished for its beauty and versatility.
