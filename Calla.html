Let's Crochet!



  • 3.5mm Crochet Hook
  • 2 Yarn Colors
  • Polyfil/ Filler
  • Glue Gun
  • Crochet Needle
  • Stitch Marker


  • mr: Magic Ring
  • slst: Slip Stitch
  • ch: Chain
  • st(s): Stitch(es)
  • sc: Single Crochet
  • hdc: Half Double Crochet
  • dc: Double Crochet

We will be working on a spiral, so weâ€™ll need a stitch marker to identify the first stitch of each round.


Round1: Magic Ring 6sc around the magic ring(6sts)
Round2: 2sc on each st around(12sts)
Round3: 1sc on one st and 2sc on next st around(18sts)
Round4: 1sc on two sts, 2sc on the next st around(24sts)
Round5: 1sc on three sts, 2sc on the next st around(30sts)
Round6-18: 1sc on each st around(30sts)
After Round18. Fasten off leaving a long tail. Add a tapestry needle to the long tail of yarn left.
Stuff with polyfil. Fold the opening as shown and sew the top.


Ch20. 1sc on the 2nd ch from the hook and on the next ch, 1hdc each on next 3 ch, 1dc each on next 9sts, on the last 5ch, 1hdc each on next 3ch, 1sc on the 19th ch, 3sc on the last ch
Working on the opposite side of the chain
1sc on the 19th ch on the opposite side, 1hdc each on the next 3ch on the opposite side, 1dc each on the next 9ch on the opposite side, 1hdc each on the next 3ch on the opposite side, 1sc each on last 2sts, slst on the starting sc. Fasten off and weave the ends.
Now place 3 skewers and use sticky tape to wrap them around in equal spacing.
Now insert the skewers to the bottom of the flower. Use hot glue gun to hold the stick in place.
Now use the same yarn with which we have worked our leaf, use hot glue and roll the yarn around the stick.
